The Gentle Hokage Family is a group of individual that met in Kumu app. It was created by group of gifters and supporters that aim to support aspiring streamers. It is where the gifters, supporters and streamers meet each other.

Objectives of GH

  • Learn how to stream
  • Learn how to make content
  • Support and Build each other
  • Help new aspiring streamer become a featured streamer
  • Build trust and friendship
  • Learn how to properly engage with streamers
    • Gentle
    • Respectful
    • Kind
    • Wholesome
    • Generous
    • Friendly to anyone
    • Caring
